Home Vacation

For my home vacation, I just stayed at home or hung out with friends. We would go to the pool or just walk around downtown Greenville. Sometimes we went to Dave & Busters and clowned around. It was really fun to just relax and talk with my friends before I had to leave Greenville and go to Myrtle Beach.

Beach Vacation

Me and my family went to Myrtle beach for our Beach Vacation. We rented a really nice condo on the Myrtle Beach Website near the beach and stayed for a couple weeks. Me and my brother even took surfing lessons, which looks a lot harder than the professionals make it seem. All in all, the vacation was really fun and I would love to go again next summer.

Job during the summer

My job during summer was working for my parents in our family-owned restaurant. The restaurant is located at Five Forks where we sell Chinese food. My normal schedule is 6 days a week for 4-5 hours each day. The job itself is quite boring and can also be infuriating if you're dealing with annoying customers. Eventually, I think I'm going to apply to Publix or Lowes to get better hours.
